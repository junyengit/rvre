Process

From application to renewal —
fully automated.

01

Applicant Submits

Prospective tenants apply online. RVRE instantly collects all required documents and consent forms via a branded portal.

02

AI Verifies

Our engine cross-checks identity, credit, employment, and rental history in seconds — delivering a clear risk score and recommendation.

03

Lease Executed

Approved tenants receive a digital lease for e-signing. All documents are stored, indexed, and accessible in the RVRE vault.

04

Renew on Autopilot

60–90 days before expiry, RVRE triggers renewal workflows, sends outreach, and closes renewals — without manual intervention.
